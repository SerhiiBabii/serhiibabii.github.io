Gllacy Store is the online and offline store selling ice cream of its own production in bulk

All our ice cream is made in our own production facility, using modern equipment and time-tested technologies.

We purchase ingredients only from trusted farms and companies with which we have long-term cooperation.

Real cream and milk of the highest quality are used to make ice cream. All additional ingredients and additives are made from natural, environmentally friendly raw materials.

Our ice cream is delivered to customers in a special thermal pack, which prevents the ice cream from melting on the way and allows it to retain its excellent taste.
